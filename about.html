Bio

I’m Dom, a user-interface and user-experience designer focused on creating great digital products. I have over 15 years experience working in the field of design. Having been formally trained in the signage and display industries and taught the art of hand crafted and hand painted signage. There my understanding of type, colour and design grew and it was where I developed my skills as a graphic designer across multiple mediums. From large format print design and production, branding initatives and more recently my focus has been digital design. These skills lead me to sales and marketing roles within corporate environments and gained experience working within both small and large teams.

Currently, my time is taken up between a remote, IT managment role and as a freelance designer and front end developer. I hold current IT network and security certifications and am always open to learning the latest trends in technology and design. Outside of all this, I am an avid mountain biker keen to conquer every trail NZ has to offer and with my partner enjoy travelling NZ in our motorhome.

My New Zealand

Create an iconic memory of your travels throughout New Zealand.

Roles

Concept creater, user research, user interviews, wireframing, prototyping, visual design and branding.

The problem

People take alot of photos while holidaying in New Zealand. But when the holiday is over, how easily or often do we look at these photos to take ourselves back to those great times?

Photographs

Many pictures are taken over the course of a road trip throughout NZ. The ease of taking digital photos make it possible to record hundreds of pictures and videos.

Storage

Most people store the pictures in the cloud, on their phone or hardrive. A few print hard copies for a photo album to then be stored on a book shelf or under a coffe table.

How might we

How might we remind the user of their holiday?

The concept

Iconic imagery to invoke a memory

An app that creates an iconic memory of what the user saw or experienced in NZ. It combines silhouette images and text to represent places, flora and fauna, walks, events etc. A print on demand service (POD) then produces the product for distribution.

How it works

The user enters information, at home for example (static) or uses the option of GPS mode completing the fields as they travel (mobile).

Static

This is an option for someone who has completed their holiday and can complete the information at home or at the end of their travels.The user can select all the key sites and names and have the print sent to family and friends overseas as a reminder of New Zealand.

Mobile

The GPS will list the recommended sites or activities to view within the current area. These are manually checked if completed. Area names are automatically entered.

The style is selected.

The style of the print is chosen and from there a mockup of the print is presented. The user can make changes here and once happy makes the payment and submits it for production.

Print on demand

The app creates and sends the file to a print on demand company that produces and ships the product.

Branding

Creating the unique

The name My New Zealand aims to communicate to the user that this app creates a product unique to them. There are many POD businesses that produce prints of New Zealand. The key difference with MYNZ is that the user determines the outcome and this produces a more meaningful product. The intention is that their artwork will create conversation, memories and may invoke them to review their photos and videos they took. They also have a cool original artwork for their wall.
